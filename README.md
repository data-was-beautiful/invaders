### Space Invaders - Requirements for LLM

Technology: C using the SDL libraries to work on Linux.

Basic game: Space invaders based on the 80s game with aliens coming down in waves from the top of the screen dropping bombs moving side to side. Three bases under which your space ship can hide. You can shoot back at the aliens.

You have three lives before the game ends and being hit by a bomb kills you. Occasionally a flying saucer moves across the top of the screen. It can also bomb and kill you.

At the very top of the screen is your current score which is based on each alien you kill. 50 points per alien. Destroying the flying saucer gets your 200 points.

The top of the screen also contains the number of lives you have remaining represented by a smaller version of the image of your ship. One icon for each life remaining. You start with three lives.

The game start with a title page where you can select to play the game or turn sound on and off. When the game due to you dying, you return to the title page after a brief message is displayed saying Game Over.

When all aliens are destroyed on a screen, the game restarts but you retain your current score and gain an extra life.

Controls are the cursor keys to move left and right. And the space bar to shoot. There is also a special power which you get randomly during each game that allows you to press enter and it sends out a powerful pulse that destroys a large number of aliens, but it also does a lot of damage to your bases.

You can pause the game by pressing P at any time. And press it again to unpause and return to the game. You can also press Q to quit immediately and return to the title screen.

The title screen has a nice artistic background showing some aliens, your craft, bullets flying around and the title of the game, Alien Invasion.
